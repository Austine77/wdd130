Nigeria

Nigeria, officially the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is located in West Africa. Covering an area of approximately 923,769 square kilometers (356,669 square miles), it is home to over 230 million people, making it the most populous country in Africa and the sixth largest in the world. The nation's capital is Abuja, while Lagos stands as its largest city and a key commercial center. Nigeria boasts a rich and varied landscape, from deserts and plains to swamps, mountains, and lush rainforests. The Niger River, one of the country’s most prominent features, plays a crucial role in supporting Nigeria’s extensive agricultural activities.
