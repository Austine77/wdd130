History

Founded in 2025 by Austine77 and based in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria, Nigeria Whitewater Rafting Adventure is your gateway to thrilling rafting experiences. From beginner-friendly rides to intense rapids, we offer adventures for all skill levels.
