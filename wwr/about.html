Welcome to Sunday Prince Augustine White water rafting! Experience the thrill of wild waters, conquer the current and discover the power of the river. Grab your paddle, embrace the rush, and let the river take you on an exhilarating ride. Adventures awaits you- let's make waves together.

History

Sunday Prince Augustine, White water rafting started in the year 2019, when a small group of river enthusiasts sets out to share their passion for outdoors and the thrill of navigating powerful waterways. Our mission has always been more than just adventure- It's about connecting with nature, fostering community and protecting the rivers we cherish. We are designed to provide you with information and resources for rafting trips, kayaking lessons, stand-up paddleboarding and multi-day river expeditions. We offer high safety measures, required gear and preparation tips for participants. Whether you're a seasoned paddler or a first-time adventurer, we invite you to become part of our story. Together let's ride the rapids, embrace the wild, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Today, the rafting with us on this white water retains a bucket-list of adventures. We offer single-day and multi-day expeditions, including overnight trips withcamping along the riverbanks. Rooted in this Nation, our company was born out from love of exploration and a commitment to preserving the wild life that inspire us. Over the years we have grown from a humble operation to one of the most trusted names in the white water rafting. Along the way we have guided thousands of thrill-seekers through some of the most scenic and challenging rapids in the region. Every bend in the river has a story to tell, and we have built our legacy on making those stories come alive for our guests. We have remaind dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for everyone who joins us on the water.
