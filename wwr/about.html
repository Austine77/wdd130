About Nigeria White Water Rafting

We craft unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures filled with excitement, thrill, and memories that will last a lifetime. Join us and embark on the journey of a lifetime.

History

Founded in 2025 by Sunday Prince Augustine and based in Akwa Ibom, Nigeria, Nigeria Whitewater Rafting Adventure is your gateway to thrilling rafting experiences. From beginner-friendly rides to intense rapids, we offer adventures for all skill levels.

Our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences while preserving the beauty of our rivers and fostering a love for outdoor adventure.
